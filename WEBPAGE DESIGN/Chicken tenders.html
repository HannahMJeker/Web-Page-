This is a recipe that you can make and it is very easy, and VERY delicious

Ingredients

  1. 1 lb of chicken tenders
  2. 1 cup of graduated parmesan
  3. 2 tablespoons of dried parsley
  4. 1 tablespoon of dried oreageno
  5. 1 tablespoon of paprika
  6. 1 teaspoon of salt
  7. 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  8. 1/2 cup of butter

DIRECTIONS

  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. combine all dry ingredients
  3. line a shallow pan with foil
  4. melt butter in a shallow dish
  5. dip each piece of butter and in in season mixture and place in a pan
  6. bake for 20-30 minuets or until chicken is doen
